About Green Society Nepal

Our journey, mission, and the dedicated team behind our work.

Our Mission

To launch effective programs in environmental conservation, health, and education by mobilizing local communities and collaborating with national and international organizations. We aim to empower marginalized groups and foster a sustainable, self-reliant society.

Our Vision

We envision a clean, green, and healthy society where nature is protected, children have access to quality education, communities are healthy, and every individual is an active steward of the environment for future generations.

Our History

Founded in 2011 by a group of passionate youths in Sauraha, Chitwan, Green Society Nepal was born out of a desire to address pressing environmental and social issues in our community. From humble beginnings organizing local clean-up drives, we have grown into a respected organization managing diverse projects, including our flagship Elephant Dung Paper enterprise, educational sponsorships, and a community health clinic. Our journey is a testament to the power of community action.
