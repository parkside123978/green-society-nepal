Welcome to Green Society Nepal

Green Society Nepal (GSN) is a non-profit, non-governmental social organization dedicated to the conservation of nature and the environment. Established in 2011 in Sauraha, Chitwan, we are committed to fostering a sustainable community through various programs in education, health, environmental protection, and empowerment. Our strength lies in our dedicated members and the support of our local and international partners.

Our Key Initiatives

Environment

Tree plantation, river clean-ups, and awareness campaigns.

Education

Sponsoring children's education and providing school supplies.

Elephant Dung Paper

An innovative, eco-friendly enterprise for conservation.
