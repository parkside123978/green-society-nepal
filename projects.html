Our Projects

Driving change through targeted, community-focused initiatives.

Environment Program

We actively engage in tree plantation, regular clean-up campaigns for rivers and public spaces, and awareness programs to promote environmental stewardship.

Elephant Dung Paper Enterprise

A flagship project that addresses human-elephant conflict by creating economic value from elephant dung. This eco-friendly paper production provides local employment and funds conservation efforts.

Education Program

We believe education is key to a brighter future. GSN sponsors the education of underprivileged children, providing them with scholarships, uniforms, and necessary school supplies.

Community Health Clinic

Our health clinic provides accessible and affordable basic health services to the local community, focusing on primary care, check-ups, and health awareness campaigns.

Local Craft Centre

To empower local women and preserve traditional skills, our craft center provides training and a marketplace for handmade goods. This initiative creates economic independence for local families.

Volunteer Program

We welcome volunteers from around the world to contribute their skills and passion. Our program offers a chance to immerse in Nepali culture while making a tangible impact in our projects.
